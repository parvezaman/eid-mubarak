Ad Dhul-Hijjhah

  • Allah SWT has delcared the importance of these days in Surah Al-Fazr verses 1-2 by taking oath of it.
  • Narrated Ibn `Abbas: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "No good deeds done on other days are superior to those done on these (first ten days of Dhul Hijja)." Then some companions of the Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Not even Jihad?" He replied, "Not even Jihad, except that of a man who does it by putting himself and his property in danger (for Allah's sake) and does not return with any of those things." (Sahih Al Bukhari 969)
  • Many of our scholars opinion is , the best of days are the first 10 days of Dhul-Hijjhah and the best of nights are the last 10 nights of Ramadan.

  • To do Tawba (istigfar) a lot
  • To perform farz and nafal salah sincerely
  • To fast in these days especially in the day of arafa
  • To perform Hajj and Umrah
  • To be in Dhikr of Allah SWT (any sort of kalimah: Subhanallah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu-Akbar etc.
  • Specified takbir in 9-13 dates
  • To do the Qurbani (Sacrifice)

Among the scholasrs there are two mentions regarding this issue:

  1. First opinion: It's Wajib. Imam Awjayi, Imam Lais, Imam Abu Hanifa and so on.
  2. Second opinion:It's Sunnah. Most of the scholars including imam malek and shafeyi have mentioned it as a sunnah. But they have also mentioned that inspite of having ability, ommision of doing Kurbani is makhruh.

  • And for every [religious] community We have appointed a rite [of sacrifice] that they may mention the name of Allah over what He has provided for them of [sacrificial] animals. For your god is one God, so to Him submit. And, [O Muḥammad], give good tidings to the humble [before their Lord] (Sura Hajj: 34)
  • Animals you can Sacrifice:
    • Camel
    • Cow/Bull
    • Buffalo
    • Goat
    • Ship
    • Dumba
    According to Imam Malik Rh. the best among the sacrificial animals is the black and white Dumba with horn.
  • Camel, Cow and Buffalo can be shared highest in 7 shares.
  • Animals must meet the age criteria:
    • camel: 5 years old (minimum)
    • Cow/Buffalo: 2 years old (minimum)
    • Others: 1 year old (minimum)
  • sacrificial anumals must be:
    • Flawless
    • Healthy
    • Nice-looking

Kurbani (Sacrifice) is a form of ibadah that is related to time-duration. Without the Specified timeframe it won't be counted as a Kurbani. The time-duration is for four (4) days as bellow;
  • 10th of Dhul-Hijjah after performing Eid-Salat
  • 11th of Dhul-Hijjah
  • 12th of Dhul-Hijjah
  • 13th of Dhul-Hijjah before sunset

One must avoid to cut, tear or break any sort of hair or nail of his/her body until he/she sacrifices.

Thus it is [what has been mentioned in the Verses (27, 28, 29, 30, 31) is an obligation that mankind owes to Allâh] and whosoever honours the Symbols of Allâh, then it is truly from the piety of the hearts. (Surah Al-Hajj: 32)
  • To say specified takbir: Allahu Akbar, Allahu Akbar, Allahu Akbar, Laa Ilaha Illallahu Allahu Akbar, Allahu Akbar, Wa Lillahil Hamd.
  • To do sacrifice
  • To take bath and put on perfumes for men
  • To eat the meat of sacrificed animal
  • To walk to Eid-gah if possible
  • To perform salah with the muslim and take part in khutbah
  • To alternate/change the way.
  • To greet people
      • Sahabahs (Ra) of the Rasul (PBUH) used to say;
    • Taqabballallahu minna wa mingkum
    • Kullu 'Aamiw wa angtum bikhairin
    • Eiduka Mubaarakan

  • To avoid any sort of haram; eg: listening to music, watching films/movies, ignoring the Porda etc.
  • Cutting, tearing or breaking any sort of hair or nail before Kurbani.
  • Making it obligatory to visit (ziyarat) burials/graveyards.
  • A woman to adopt/copy a man any sort of way (way of speaking, dressing etc.) and vice versa.
  • Any sort of waste and transgression.
